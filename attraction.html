Welcome To a Land Of Magic

Get ready for your visit to Wonderworld

All Attractions

Wizard's Whirl

Brace yourself for a spellbinding adventure as the Wizard levitates you in a mesmerizing pendulum swing! Hold on tight and experience the magic!

Fantasia Flyer

Lift off and soar 150 feet into the sky, where you’ll be enchanted by breathtaking panoramic views of Wonderworld. Experience the wonder of flight and capture unforgettable moments from the best seat in the realm!

Dragon's Descent

Plunge through the fire and swoop around sharp turns before dropping into desolate canyons. Experience the thrill of a dragon’s fury on this heart-pounding adventure!

Starry Steeds

Sit astride a regal horse and gently 'gallop' through a whirling backdrop of color and sound. Let the celestial melodies and twinkling lights carry you on a magical journey through realms of wonder and delight

Knight's Kascade

Embark on a daring quest with this thrilling wet rollercoaster ride designed especially for brave young adventurers! Join the knights of the realm as you plunge into excitement and adventure amidst cascading waters and enchanted landscapes.

Princess Parade

Join the Princess's tea party and spin round and round in a giant teacup during a music-filled whirlwind. Let the enchanting melodies and swirling colors whisk you away on a royal adventure fit for a princess!

Shops and Restaurants

King's Tavern

Step into a world of medieval charm and culinary delight, where the grandeur of a royal feast awaits. Our tavern offers a hearty menu inspired by ancient recipes, served in a majestic setting fit for a king.

Grand Emporium

Explore a treasure trove of unique souvenirs, enchanting trinkets, and magical keepsakes that capture the spirit of your adventure. From handcrafted jewelry to whimsical toys and medieval-themed collectibles, the Grand Emporium has the perfect memento for every adventurer.

Explore More Shops and Restaurants!

Embark on a culinary and shopping adventure throughout our fantasy theme park! To discover all the magical locations and plan your visit with ease, download our mobile app to view a detailed map, get exclusive offers, and never miss out on a hidden gem.

Top Things to Know

Theme Park Reservations

You’ll need both valid admission and park reservations to visit the parks and can make reservations by using our theme park reservation system.

Rules

To ensure your safety and enjoyment, please adhere to and follow all posted rules and guidelines when riding attractions. Your cooperation helps us maintain a secure and memorable experience for everyone.

Courtesy

We ask all who come to this happy place to treat others with respect, kindness and compassion. Please respect our cast members who are doing their best to provide an enjoyable experience to the guests.

Download the Official App

Seamlessly plan your visit, access mobile ordering, and enjoy more convenience than ever before. Stay in the loop with real-time updates on events and attractions, and check waiting times on the fly.

Health & Safety

Health Acknowledgement

We remain intentional in our commitment to health and safety and will continue to operate with this in mind. Guests agree to abide by any and all applicable health-and-safety requirements.

Be Aware of Your Surroundings

Stay aware of your surroundings, avoid rushing, and enjoy each moment without haste. Your safety is paramount, so let's navigate together with care

Mind the Heat

Remember to hydrate frequently and shield yourself from the sun's ray with suncreen. Stay cool, stay safe, and keep the fun going!

Know Your Medical History

Some rides may pose risks for certain conditions. Prioritize your well-being and consult with a medical professional if you have any concerns. Your safety is our top priority!
