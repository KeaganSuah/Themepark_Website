FAQs

Tickets

Tickets can be purchased from ticket booths at the entrance. Tickets are also available for advance purchase, please it check out here. Children below 3 are free. Present your child's ID or passport for verification purposes.

Food and Beverage

We offer a wide variety of restaurant choices from Asian to Western cuisines. All our menu items are prepared with fresh ingredients and have vegetarian options. Each restaurant can also cater to special dietary needs. Please reach out to the restaurant chefs for any special requests.

Height and Age Restriction

For safety reasons, admission to the attractions is restricted by age and height requirements. Please check the height and age guideline located at the rides or attractions area.
